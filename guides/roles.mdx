Your subscription role determines which account types you can generate.

## Role Hierarchy

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Free | Default tier, limited access |
| Premium | Paid subscription with expanded access |
| Ultra | Highest tier with full access |
| Resell | Special tier for resellers |

## Access Matrix

| Account Type | Free | Premium | Ultra | Resell |
|--------------|------|---------|-------|--------|
| alt | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| +30 days old | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| +1 year old | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| 5+ years old | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ |
| dump | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ |
| dump (unchecked) | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |

## Role-Specific Features

### Free
- Access to alt accounts only
- 2-hour cooldown between generations
- 100 accounts per day limit

### Premium
- Access to alt, aged, and unchecked dump accounts
- Reduced cooldowns (30s - 5m depending on type)
- 100 accounts per day limit

### Ultra
- Full access to all account types
- Minimal cooldowns (10s - 10m depending on type)
- 100 accounts per day limit

### Resell
- Alt accounts only at special pricing ($0.0013)
- No cooldowns
- No daily limit

Visit your [dashboard](https://bloxgen.net/dashboard) to upgrade your subscription and unlock more account types.
